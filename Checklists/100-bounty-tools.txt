Notes
==========================
Most tools are language‑specific (Go, Python, Rust). Check repo READMEs for install steps.

Combine reconnaissance (Subfinder/Amass) → triage (httpx) → crawling (Katana) → content discovery (ffuf/feroxbuster) → templated scanning (Nuclei) → manual validation.

Keep wordlists fresh (SecLists, commonspeak2).
